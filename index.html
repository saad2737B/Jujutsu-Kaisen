Jujutsu Kaisen




About:

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy anime and manga series created by Gege Akutami. It follows Yuji Itadori, a high schooler who gets pulled into the world of curses—twisted spirits born from human negativity. After consuming a powerful cursed object, Yuji becomes the host of Ryomen Sukuna, the King of Curses. He joins the Tokyo Jujutsu High School, where sorcerers train to fight curses and protect humanity. The story blends intense action, emotional depth, and complex characters, featuring epic battles, supernatural powers, and moral dilemmas. With standout characters like Gojo Satoru and arcs like the Shibuya Incident, JJK has quickly become one of the most iconic series of modern anime.

Where To Start?

  1. Start with Season 1 (Episodes 1–24):
    Meet Yuji Itadori, Sukuna, Gojo, and dive into the basics of jujutsu sorcery.

  2. Then Watch the Prequel Movie – Jujutsu Kaisen 0:
    3. It introduces Yuta Okkotsu and sets the stage for deeper lore. Watch after Season 1 for the best experience.

  3. Continue with Season 2:
    4. Split into two arcs — Gojo’s Past Arc and the epic Shibuya Incident Arc. Get ready for high-stakes action and emotional twists.

  4. Manga Readers:
    5. After Season 2, continue from Chapter 138 of the manga if you want to skip ahead.

Main Charecters:

  1. Yuji Itadori:
    • The main protagonist.

    • Sukuna’s vessel after swallowing a cursed finger.

    • Kind-hearted, athletic, and determined to save people — even if it costs his life.

  2. Satoru Gojo
    3.
    • The strongest jujutsu sorcerer.

    • Wields the Limitless and Six Eyes, making him nearly unbeatable.

    • Charismatic, goofy, and terrifyingly powerful — a fan favorite.

  3. Megumi Fushiguro
    4.
    • Stoic sorcerer from the Zenin clan.

    • Uses Ten Shadows Technique to summon shikigami.

      • Calm and serious, but deeply values protecting others.

  4. Nobara Kugisaki
    5.
    • Bold, fashionable, and fearless.

    • Uses Straw Doll Technique to fight with nails and a hammer.

    • Breaks the mold of your typical anime heroine — loud and proud.

  5. Ryomen Sukuna
    6.
    • The King of Curses.

    • Resides inside Yuji’s body after he eats his finger.

    • Cruel, arrogant, and extremely powerful — a major threat to all.

  6. Yuta Okkotsu
    7.
    • Protagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen 0.

    • Formerly haunted by Rika, a powerful cursed spirit.

    • One of the most promising sorcerers with immense potential.

  7. Suguru Geto
    8.
    • Former jujutsu sorcerer turned curse user.

    • Believes only jujutsu users deserve to live.

    • Once Gojo’s best friend — now one of his greatest enemies.

  8. Toji Fushiguro
  • Megumi’s father.

  • Known as the Sorcerer Killer — a non-curse user who slays jujutsu sorcerers.

  • Has superhuman physical abilities and no cursed energy.
